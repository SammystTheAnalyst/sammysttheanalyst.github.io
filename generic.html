About Sammyst The Analyst

My name is Samuel Chidiebere Nnamani a.k.a Sammyst The Analyst. I am Igbo by tribe and a Nigerian by nationality. I am a Microbiologist by discipline(BSc Applied Microbiology), Enugu State University of Science and Technology(ESUT), Enugu state, Nigeria, and a Data Analyst by profession. Ever since my early years as a pupil in school, I discovered I love Mathematics, and anything having to do with data/number calculation ignites an unquantifiable passion to provide solution to any Mathematical problem I come across. That passion and zeal for 'playing' with numbers and data informed my decision to delve into the IT field of Data Analytics.

In my role as a Data Analyst, I focus on data from two(2) fields;
1. Healthcare; This is in reference to my discipline as a Microbiologist, so I have been exposed to healthcare data.
2. Business; Having spent more than three(3) years as Store Manager in my dad's grocery store, with job requirements having to with purchase of grocery stocks from wholesalers, sales of grocery products, record keeping and decision-making process for alloting price to commodity, I have gotten used to the business terrain and use of 'business language'.
